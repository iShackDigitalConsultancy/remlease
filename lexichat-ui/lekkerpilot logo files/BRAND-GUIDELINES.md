## Typography

- **Wordmark font**: Helvetica Neue Medium (500) — substitute: Arial, system sans-serif
- **Tagline font**: Helvetica Neue Regular (400), letter-spacing 2.5–3px, uppercase
- **Wordmark treatment**: All lowercase "rem-leases" — never capitalise
- **Separation**: The dot (.) between "pilot" and "ai" is always gold

---

## Clear Space & Minimum Size

- **Clear space**: Maintain a minimum margin equal to the height of the "l" in "lekker" on all sides
- **Minimum horizontal lockup width**: 200px (digital), 50mm (print)
- **Minimum icon mark size**: 24px (digital), 8mm (print)
- **Minimum favicon size**: 16x16px

---

## Don'ts

- Don't capitalise the wordmark (no "LekkerPilot" or "LEKKERPILOT")
- Don't change the colour relationship (teal for document, gold for wing)
- Don't rotate or distort the icon mark
- Don't place the full-colour logo on busy photographic backgrounds — use mono versions
- Don't add drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the logo
- Don't separate the dot from "pilot" or "ai"
